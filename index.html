Dr. Kejing Yin is a Research Assistant Professor at the Department of Computer Science at Hong Kong Baptist University. Prior to that, he was a Post-doctoral Research Fellow under the supervision of Prof. William K. Cheung. Kejing obtained the Ph.D. degree from the same department and the Bachelorâ€™s degree from South China University of Technology. He was a visiting PhD student at the College of Computing at Georgia Institute of Technology, supervised by Prof. Jimeng Sun, from Sep. 2019 to Feb. 2020.

He is working on machine learning for high-dimensional healthcare data analytics. He is particularly interested in computational phenotyping and predictive analytics for large-scale electronic health records (EHR) data. He serves as a program committee member or reviewer for international conferences, including AAAI, IJCAI, NeurIPS, ICLR, and ICHI.
