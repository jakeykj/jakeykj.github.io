Openings at Prof. William Cheung's Research Group

Jun 28, 2018 2 min read

The research group led by Prof. William Cheung at the Department of Computer Science, Hong Kong Baptist University is recruiting Research Assistants and PhD Students. Opportunities for postdoctoral fellows are also available. Successful applicants are expected to work closely with Prof. William Cheung and his research group on projects related to AI, machine learning and computer vision for healthcare.

Prof. William Cheung is currently a Professor of the Department of Computer Science, Hong Kong Baptist University. His current research interests include artificial intelligence, data mining, collaborative information filtering, social network analysis, and healthcare informatics. For detailed information about Prof. Cheung, please visit https://www.comp.hkbu.edu.hk/v1/?page=profile&id=william.
